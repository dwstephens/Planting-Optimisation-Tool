# Uses

* Timber forest products
* Non-timber forest products (NTFPs)

    * Species
        *  Eucalyptus urophylla (Ai‑ru) for eucalyptus oil for antiseptic, flavor, and agrochemical markets.
        *  Eucalyptus alba (Ai‑bubur)  for essential oil for medicinal, cosmetic use (pesticidal, fragrance).
        *  Sterculia foetida (Ai‑nitas – Java Olive) for oil used in soap or biodiesal 
        *  Tamarindus indica (Tamarind) for pulp and seed-oil
        *  Azadirachta indica (Ai‑nimba/Sumear Malae - Neem) for fresh vegetable markets
        *  Parkia speciosa (Petai) for fresh vegetable markets
        *  Terminalia catappa (Ketapang) for edible nuts and culinary oil
        *  Calophyllum inophyllum (Samplon) for high-value skincare oils
        *  Aleurites moluccanus (Candlenut/Ai-Kamii) for high-value skincare oils
        *  Canarium vulgare (Kiar) for edible nuts and culinary oil
        *  Mangifera indica (mango) for fresh fruit markets

# Agroforestry

* Types

    * Block planting -  establishment of trees in compact, contiguous patches or woodlots within a farming landscape
    * Boundary planting - trees may serve as windbreaks, live fences, or property markers, and frequently provide additional outputs such as fodder, firewood, fruits, or medicinal products
        * Suitable Species
            *  sterculia
            *  teak
            *  eucalypts
            *  neem
            *  toona
            *  peltophorum
            *  tamarind
            * casuarina.
    * Intercropping -  integration of trees and crops on the same land unit, arranged in a mixed spatial pattern.
        * Species
            *  Casuarina equisetifolia
            *  Syzygium aromaticum
            *  Cocos nucifera
            *  Artocarpus altilis
            *  Swietenia macrophylla
            *  Coffea
            *  Selenicereus monacanthus
            *  Santalum album

        * Long term species
            * Pterocarpus indicus
            * Albizia procera
            * Falcataria falcata
            * Neolamarckia cadamba **needs wide spacing

        * Short term species
            *  Casuarina equisetifolia
            *  Santalum album
            *  Swietenia macrophylla
            *  Aleurites moluccanus
            *  Peltophorum pterocarpum
            *  Intsia bijuga
            *  Calophyllum inophyllum
            *  eucalypts

        * Not suiteable
            *  Alstonia scholaris
            *  Toona ciliata
    * Mosaic agroforestry - interspersed patches of trees, crops, and sometimes fallow or forest areas.
